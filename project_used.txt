**Project: Conversational Chatbot for Customer Service Automation**

This project involves developing a conversational AI chatbot using Large Language Models (LLMs) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to automate customer service inquiries. The chatbot will be trained to understand and respond to frequent customer queries, such as order tracking, returns, and product information, freeing human customer support agents to focus on more complex issues. The chatbot will utilize Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide accurate and up-to-date information, improving customer satisfaction and reducing support tickets.
**Project Idea: Conversational Dialogue System for Customer Service**

This project involves designing and developing a conversational AI model using Large Language Models (LLMs) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) techniques to create a customer service chatbot. The system would be trained on a dataset of common customer inquiries and responses, allowing it to generate human-like replies to user queries. The model would be integrated with a natural language processing (NLP) pipeline to handle intent identification, entity extraction, and context understanding, enabling the chatbot to provide accurate and personalized support to customers. The goal is to automate customer service operations, reducing response times and improving user experience.
